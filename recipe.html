Recipe #1 Bread

  • Time: 50 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
yeast 1/4 ounce
water 2 cups
sugar 3 tablespoons
salt 1 tablespoon
Canola Oil 2 tablespoons
flour 6 cups

Step 1: Mix all wet ingredients in a bowl. Then combine the dry with them.

Step 2: Then Knead the mixture until smooth and elastic. And place in oven pans

Step 3: Then bake in the oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Recipe #3 Hard tack

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
flour 4 cups
water 2 cups
sugar 1 tablespoons
salt 4 tablespoon
yeast 1 tablespoons

Step 1: Mix all wet ingredients in a bowl. Then combine the dry with them.

Step 2: Mold the new mixture into a long strip and cut into small stars

Step 3: Then bake in the oven at 350 degrees for 21 minutes.
