Recipe #1

  • Time: 50 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
yeast 1/4 ounce
water 2 cups
sugar 3 tablespoons
salt 1 tablespoon
Canola Oil 2 tablespoons
flour 6 cups

Step 1: Mix all wet ingredients in a bowl. Then combine the dry with them.

Step 2: Then Knead the mixture until smooth and elastic. And place in oven pans

Step 3: Then bake in the oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
